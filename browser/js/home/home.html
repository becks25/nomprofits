Nomprofits

Dinners with people who care.

The goal of Nomprofits is simple: to connect young professionals to non-profit organizations that are doing the work we care about, so that we can support them in making our world a better one.

And what could be better than learning about those non-profits over a delicious dinner? Bringing people together over a meal is one of the most effective ways to build relationships. Each Nomprofits dinner will be intimate, informative and fun and our goal is that each participant will walk away with a connection to the non-profits that present (and new friends to boot).

For the cost of a night out, you'll get:

  • One tasty multi-course meal
  • Incredible conversation
  • A deeper understanding of the work happening in the causes you care about
  • New friends
  • A really fun time (we're pulling out a lot of stops)

...and did we mention a tasty multi-course meal?
